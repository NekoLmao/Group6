SANJALIKA WATER WORLD

WaterWorld is a one-of-a-kind Water Park offering fun the entire family will enjoy. We have everything you could want for an exciting and entertaining experience.

Challenge 36 game devices to enjoy the wonderful feeling of each game. Not only do you have fun, but games are also seen as challenges. Overcoming all 36 challenges of the game, you will be stronger when facing the challenges in life.

Sanjalika Park Water Park is not only one of the familiar entertainment centers of the city people, but also a place to bring comfort and entertainment to children after hard study days. Your baby's day off will be more fun when he and his parents have fun with the family of Elephant, .. where the colorful childhood paradise is full of slides for babies.

Coming to Sanjalika Park Water Park, you will really feel secure when participating in games at the park because clean water is regularly renewed, fresh air and there is always a professional rescue team ready to assist, take care you. In addition, the park also has a system of fast food stalls and a 400-seat restaurant with a variety of deliciously prepared menus ready to serve you.
