Activities

In Sanjalika amusement park, there is a water park equipped with a wave pool, Tiki waterfall combined with Big Kanuha Swimming Pool, forming a combination of water games that create excitement for visitors. The games include: water guns, slides, fountains, caps ... The miniature sea corner with a gentle slope is where visitors can immerse themselves in the clear blue water, play with each wave. Hidden in the image of a Polynesian village, is the Tiki Hut stage and the Tiki Bar area - the venue for performing arts repertoire and serving typical cuisine, offering visitors flavorful dishes. tropical coast ... Art and food repertoire are reproduced in accordance with the spirit of living in harmony with nature of the Polynesian ethnic group.

THRILLING GAMES

For this game, visitors will experience different thrills such as cable slide, free jump. Next to it was swinging down the steep cliffs, the water was pouring down. Especially, moving along the rocky shore pushes danger and hardship. However, you will have some peace of mind when throughout the journey, players will always accompany the instructors. They are experienced with these challenges and will be of great help.
